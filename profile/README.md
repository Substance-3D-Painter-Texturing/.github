## 🧭 What It Is
Substance 3D Painter for Windows is Adobe's application for painting textures directly onto 3D models. You load a mesh, drop materials onto it, and refine every surface while a physically based viewport shows exactly how the asset will look under real lighting. It replaces tedious 2D texture-sheet editing with an interactive, layered approach where smart materials and masks do the heavy lifting.

> With substance painter 3d, the model in the viewport is your canvas — brush strokes and materials resolve on the surface as you work, so what you see is what you export.

## ✨ Key Features
- 🖌️ **Paint on the model** — Apply color, roughness, metalness, and height directly to the 3D surface instead of guessing on flat maps.
- 🧱 **Smart materials & masks** — Reusable, procedural materials and masks adapt to any geometry and add wear and grime automatically.
- 👁️ **Real-time PBR viewport** — Watch physically based results update live so the in-app preview matches your final render.
- 🗂️ **Non-destructive layers** — Build up detail with stacked layers, filters, and generators you can adjust at any time.
- 📦 **Engine-ready export** — Bake maps and export texture sets with presets for popular game engines and film pipelines.

## 🎯 First Steps
1. Start a new project and import a 3D model into the workspace.
2. Bake your mesh maps so smart masks and generators understand the geometry.
3. Assign a smart material to establish a believable base surface.
4. Layer in hand-painted detail and masks, checking the live viewport as you go.
5. Export a texture set using the preset that matches your renderer or engine.

## ❓ Questions
**Is Substance 3D Painter free?**
It is a paid Adobe application, but it comes with a free trial so you can evaluate it first. After the trial you can continue with a subscription or perpetual license; there is no free full version.

**Which versions of Windows are supported?**
Substance 3D Painter is a 64-bit Windows application. Use a current build of Windows 10 or Windows 11 with updated GPU drivers for the best results.

**Is my data secure?**
Projects and exported maps live on your own Windows PC, and sign-in runs through Adobe's account system. Keep the app and drivers current and back up your project files.
